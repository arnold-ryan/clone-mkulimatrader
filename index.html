FAQ
Common questions
Everything you need to know before getting started.
Is Mkulima Trader free to use? ▼
Yes, completely free. All tools — the Digit Analyser, Bot Builder, Manual Trader, and Classic Trader — cost nothing to use. You only need a Deriv account to execute real trades.
Do I need coding skills? ▼
No. The bot builder uses a visual drag-and-drop interface. AI strategies load with a single click. The digit analyser just runs in your browser — nothing to install or configure.
What is the minimum stake? ▼
The minimum stake for most strategies is $0.35 USD. Accumulator strategies start from $1.00. These limits are set by Deriv, not by Mkulima Trader.
Is this an official Deriv product? ▼
Mkulima Trader is an independent third-party platform built on Deriv's official API. It is not owned or operated by Deriv. All trades are executed directly through your Deriv account.
Can I use Mkulima Trader on my phone? ▼
Yes. All platforms are fully responsive and work on mobile browsers. No app download needed — just open your browser and go.
Are the AI strategies guaranteed to make profit? ▼
No. Trading involves risk and past performance does not guarantee future results. AI strategies are tools to assist your trading decisions, not guarantees of profit. Always use appropriate risk management settings and trade only what you can afford to lose.
